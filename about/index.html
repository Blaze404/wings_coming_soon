Wings 2020

Intelligence With Ambition

WINGS is the national level technical festival of the Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad. We at GECA have been hosting this prestigious event for over 10 years now and have seen participants from various parts of the nation. Aurangabad is the house of many huge companies and industry leaders. Keeping this in mind, we have competitions that are industry oriented and nourish one’s in-demand skills. With better and bigger events on board this year, we are expecting a footfall of about 6000 enthusiastic, brilliant young minds. This year being the Diamond Jubilee of our esteemed college the celebration becomes even grander and fun-filled.

As an effective platform for new ideas and fruitful collaborations, we at WINGS believe this offers our partners an excellent opportunity to reach out and communicate with a larger audience. We assure you that your company will be well-represented in WINGS 2020, and hope that you will decide to partner with us.

THE TEAM

At Rally, we believe that we can't do our best work without a chance to recharge occasionally. A few times a year, we need to step away from our desks and completely disconnect. So in 2016, we created Rally Camp - a bi-annual camping retreat open to anyone in the industry. We pick the location, spread the word and provide the beer. That’s it. There are no lectures and no agendas...just like-minded people hanging out in the great outdoors.

These Rally Camps have become an important part of our company culture. It strengthens our team, creates epic memories, and gives us the opportunity to make new connections. It’s amazing what fresh air and a break from our screens does for the mind.
