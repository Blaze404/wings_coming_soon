CYCLOWAY

What is CYCLO WAY?

It comprises of analyzing and designing of roller coaster model that operates like a railroad track. They are made from steel or wood and comprises a series of hill and drops, sudden turns and loops like element. Roller coaster don’t have engine. Team shall design and build a Roller coaster meeting requirement specified in these rules. An overview about the event is provided here and the problem statement will be provided at the time of competition.

  • Maximum team size is 5
  • Participants can form teams from different branches/ college/ university/ institute.
  • No two teams must have any common member
  • Teams are not allowed to touch their model once the ride begins
  • The one day time limit for the completion of design, one day for model making each day 360 minutes
  • Size restrictions - the height should not be more than 2m
  • The model should be designed for a regular size, steel or glass marble
  • The starting position at the top of the first hill should be clearly marked
  • You will have to measure the total track length and mention it somewhere on the track
  • Your team may use more than one marble
  • The use of electricity during the competition is prohibited
  • Magnets are permitted without restrictions. However, electromagnets are prohibited
  • Only gravity and magnetism may be used to propel the “COASTER”
  • The decision of the judges shall be final. Any coaster that violates the rule above or the spirit of the competition will be disqualified.
