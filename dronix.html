DRONIX

What is DRONIX?

DRONIX is an event of WINGS2k20. The main task of this competition is to design a rotary wing UAV to carry and drop payload at designated area through an obstacle course.

  • Flight time will be counted from the moment vehicle leaves ground and lands again after completing the task
  • After a crash or ground touch vehicle will have to start from last checkpoint it had travelled
  • Maximum time of 5 minutes will be allowed for completion of the course after which total marks will be calculated according to methodology given in scoring section
  • Only registrations done through official wings website are valid. Spot entry can be availed only through online means
  • Up to 5 members are allowed per team
  • No team will be allowed to perform test runs on actual arena
  • Every team can participate only once. Multiple attempts by same vehicle are not allowed
  • Commercially available drones like DJI phantoms are will not be allowed to compete
  • Time keeping done by participants will not be considered valid
  • In case of any disputes, organizers’ decision will be considered final
  • Organizers reserve the rights to change any of the above rules without prior notice
  • Any damage sustained to vehicle during the competition will not compensated by organizers

Rules.

Round 1

  • Every obstacle has specific points assigned to it according to the difficulty level which will awarded to the team after the obstacle is successfully completed with payload still attached to the vehicle.
  • A team may choose to skip some of the obstacles which are not mandatory. No penalty will be applied for such practice in first round
  • Time bonus will be awarded to the teams which will complete course with time to spare
  • Up to five crashes or ground touches will not be penalized. Thereafter a penalty of 10 points will be imposed after each such infraction

Round 2

  • Top one third of all participating teams in first round will be eligible for participation in second round which will be held on next day.
  • Obstacle density as well difficulty will be increased in second round.
  • If a team chooses to skip some of the obstacles, it will be penalized one fourth the original points for that obstacle
  • The scoring system will be same as in the first round except number of crashes and ground touches not penalized will be three. Thereafter a penalty of 10 points will be imposed after each such infraction.
  • An additional obstacle involving simple image processing will be the last obstacle of second round.

Round 3

  • Based on any given theme participant have to design web page. (Template or minimum required source will be provided to students).
  • Duration: 120 Minutes
