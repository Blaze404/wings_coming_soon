What is CICADA 3301?

CICADA 3301 is a mysterious puzzle solving competition with your technical knowledge. Participants require a basic knowledge of programming languages. CICADA 3301 is an event where you have to utilise your technical knowledge to solve mysterious puzzles to uncover the treasure

  • There will be individual participation.
  • One participant can only have single entry
  • Every round will be an elimination round.
  • The participants to complete the task in minimum time will go to the next round.
  • The participants should be present at the venue 30min prior of the given time for each round to avoid any hustle and bustle.

Rules.

Round 1

  • The participants have to solve 3 mysterious digital puzzles.
  • The solution of each puzzle will contain the clue of the next puzzle.
  • The one to solve it in minimum time will go to the next round.

Round 2

  • The participants have to solve a given problem statement.
  • The one to solve it in minimum time will go to the next round.

Round 3

  • Round 3 will be a Surprise Round.
